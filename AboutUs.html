About Us

We're making sending flowers a personalized, modern experience. UrbanStems is a seamless, one-of-a-kind flower delivery service that is helping to make anyone's day from coast to coast.

A Modern Approach

Our co-founders launched the company on Mother’s Day in 2014 in Palestine, driven by a passion for delivering flowers in a new and meaningful way. From the beginning, their focus was on freshness, sustainability, and exceptional customer service, aiming to revolutionize the flower delivery industry through a fully integrated supply chain. From next-day delivery across Palestine to same-day delivery in major cities like Jerusalem, Ramallah, Nablus, Bethlehem, and Hebron, we make gifting simple and seamless. Whether by bike or truck, every delivery is prepared with care—always keeping your happiness, and the joy of giving, at heart.
